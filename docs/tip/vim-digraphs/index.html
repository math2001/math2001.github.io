Vim digraphs

  • cool feature
  • vim
  • symbols

This is a pretty good thing if you use symbols that aren’t on your keyboard, like the copyright symbol for example (¬©).

Do you know how much time it took me to insert this symbol? Less than a second… Do you know how much time it would have took you? Well, depending on your internet connection, it might be about 10 seconds.

So, how did I do that? I just pressed ctr+k+Co (in insert mode).

ctrl+k in insert mode listens for a shortcut, which will then insert the corresponding symbol.

How do you know which one it is? Just look for it in the :digraphs list!

You can also define your own digraph:

:digraph .. ‚Ä¶

You can also specify the decimal value of the symbol, like so:

:diagraph .. 8230
